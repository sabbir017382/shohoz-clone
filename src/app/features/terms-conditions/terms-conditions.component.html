PASSENGER PROTECTION PLAN (THE SERVICE)

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Accidental Death Benefit: For the purpose of an insurance policy under this Agreement, an accidental death is any death that results from an accident or external violence... accidental death benefit shall mean Tk. 2.25 lac payable to the legal successor/nominee...

Death benefit: Death Benefit is the amount on a life insurance policy... death benefit shall mean Tk. 50,000/- payable within 07 days...

EXCLUSION FOR Natural/Accidental Death:

Claim shall not be payable if death is caused by the following actions:

  • attempted suicide or intentionally self-inflicted injury;
  • strikes, riots, civil commotion, war;
  • military service in war time;
  • committing a felony;
  • racing on wheels;
  • after age 70 years (natural death only);
  • alcohol or drug affected accident;
  • aircraft travel except commercial passenger airline;

Minor injuries: If injury meets definition of minor injury...

  • Sprains
  • Strains
  • Bruises
  • Cuts
  • Road rash
  • Mild concussions

Major Injuries: A major injury may lead to death or disability.

  • Compound fractures
  • Head or eye injury
  • Gunshot wounds
  • Severe burns

Claim: Insurance claim is a formal request... claim shall be settled within 5-7 working days...

Claim Documentation
For Natural Deaths:
  • Death Certificate
  • Proof of Age (NID/Passport)
  • Original Bus Ticket
For Accidental Deaths:
  • Police report (Surtohal)
  • Death Certificate
  • Proof of Age
For Hospitalization:
  • Doctor’s Advice
  • Discharge Certificate
